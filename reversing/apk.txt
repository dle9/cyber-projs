# android manifest
- defines key information for the apk - permissions, filters, entry points

# intent filters
- interprocess communication 
- intents are broadcasted on a device
- to receive a broadcasted intent, app creates an intent filter
- explicit intent: specifies which app to handle it
- implicit intent: app must be registered to receive the intent
